
eventStartEditable *1.6.3*
==========================

Allow events' start times to be editable through dragging.

<div class='spec' markdown='1'>
Boolean, *default*: `true`
</div>

This option can be overridden on a per-source basis with the
`startEditable` [Event Source Object](../event_data/Event_Source_Object) option or on a per-event basis with the
`startEditable` [Event Object](../event_data/Event_Object) option.
